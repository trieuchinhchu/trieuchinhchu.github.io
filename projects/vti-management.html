Project Overview

Developed a comprehensive internal management system for VTI Corporation, an IT outsourcing and digital transformation company. The ERP system covered 7 core HR and finance modules, automating nearly 100% of the HRM flow — from Recruitment → Onboarding → Attendance → Timesheet → Payroll. This enabled employees to self-verify their timesheets and payslips, reducing HR/Accounting workload by 99%.

Key Contributions

ERP

Developed 7 ERP modules: Payroll, Contracts, Employees, Accounting, Training, Attendance, Leave.

HRM

Automated nearly 100% of the HRM flow: Recruitment → Onboarding → Attendance → Timesheet → Payroll. Employees self-verify their timesheets and payslips, reducing HR/Accounting department workload by 99%.

DevOps

Implemented CI pipeline using GitLab CI + Sonarqube for automated code quality checks and deployment.
